Virtual CISO

Secure Your Information with An Effective Security Strategy

A Virtual Chief Information Security Officer is someone that provides you with a service that enables your business to implement an effective security program and cyber security strategy, helping you strengthen organizational capabilities while greatly reducing business risk.

Why do you need a virtual CISO?
A virtual CISO is for businesses that do not have their own management executive to drive the information security initiatives and governance and want support in improving their cyber defense systems. A virtual CISO can also be highly beneficial to businesses that have an IT team but wish to understand and improve their cyber defense systems. 24/7 Techies provides you with an experienced virtual CISO to help you analyze your current cyber environment, implement security strategies, and protect business-critical information.
